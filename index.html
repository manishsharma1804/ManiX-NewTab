Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Manix.

Is Manix completely free? +
Yes! Manix is completely free with no hidden costs, premium tiers, or subscription fees. All features are available to everyone.
Does Manix collect my data? +
Absolutely not. Manix operates entirely offline and doesn't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is our top priority.
How do I customize my shortcuts? +
Simply click the settings icon on your new tab page. You can add, edit, or remove shortcuts with just a few clicks. Drag and drop to reorder them.
Will Manix slow down my browser? +
No! Manix is built for performance with minimal resource usage. It's optimized to load instantly and won't affect your browser's speed.
Can I sync my settings across devices? +
Yes! Your Manix settings automatically sync across all your Chrome browsers when you're signed into your Google account.
