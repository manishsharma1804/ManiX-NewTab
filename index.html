Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ManiX.

How do I install Manix? +
Simply visit the Chrome Web Store and click "Add to Chrome". The extension will install automatically and you'll see the beautiful Manix interface the next time you open a new tab.
Is Manix completely free? +
Yes! Manix is completely free with no hidden costs, premium tiers, or subscription fees. All features are available to everyone.
Does Manix collect my data? +
Absolutely not. Manix operates entirely offline and doesn't collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is our top priority.
How do I customize my shortcuts? +
Click the settings icon on your new tab page. You can add, edit, or remove shortcuts with just a few clicks. Drag and drop to reorder them.
Will Manix slow down my browser? +
No! Manix is built for performance with minimal resource usage. It's optimized to load instantly and won't affect your browser's speed.
Can I sync my settings across devices? +
Yes! You can export your settings from one device and import them on another. Use the Export/Import feature in the settings panel to create a backup file (.json) that you can transfer and use on other systems. No Google account login required.
How do I change wallpapers? +
Click the settings icon (gear icon) on your new tab page, then go to the Background Image section. You can choose from static wallpapers, live wallpapers, or upload your own images. You can also enable auto wallpaper change at different intervals.
What browsers support Manix? +
Manix is currently available for Google Chrome and other Chromium-based browsers like Microsoft Edge, Brave, and Opera. It's designed specifically for Chrome's extension ecosystem.
How do I access the settings panel? +
Click the gear icon (⚙️) in the top-right corner of your new tab page to open the settings sidebar. Here you can customize wallpapers, set your name, configure weather, adjust display preferences, and manage backups.
How does the weather feature work? +
Enter your city name in the Location & Weather settings. ManiX will fetch real-time weather data and display it alongside the date. You can also use auto-detect location or manually refresh the weather data.
