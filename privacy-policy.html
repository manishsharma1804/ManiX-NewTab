Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how ManiX Chrome Extension ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

ManiX is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for the extension to function:

1.1 Local Storage Data

  • User Preferences: Your custom settings, themes, and layout preferences
  • Personal Information: Your name for personalized greetings
  • Location Data: City name for weather information (optional)
  • Display Settings: Time format (12/24 hour), greeting display, search bar visibility, navigation menu preferences
  • Background Settings: Wallpaper preferences, blur settings, auto wallpaper change intervals
  • Bookmarks: Access to your Chrome bookmarks for display in the new tab page
  • Search History: Recent searches for quick access (stored locally only)
  • Custom Shortcuts: Your personalized quick access links and shortcuts
  • Todo Items: Your task list and completed task history
  • Uploaded Content: Custom wallpapers and images you upload

1.2 No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as:

  • Your email address or contact information
  • Browsing history or website content
  • Personal files or documents (except uploaded wallpapers)
  • Financial information or passwords
  • Precise location coordinates (only city names are stored)

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Display your personalized new tab page with custom greetings
  • Show weather information for your specified location
  • Apply your chosen themes, wallpapers, and customizations
  • Remember your display preferences (time format, search bar visibility, etc.)
  • Manage your todo list and shortcuts
  • Display your bookmarks and recent searches
  • Provide auto wallpaper change functionality
  • Enable backup and restore of your settings

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API. No data is sent to external servers or cloud storage.

3.1 Security Measures

  • All data is encrypted using Chrome's secure storage mechanisms
  • No network transmission of personal data
  • Data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the extension
  • Uploaded wallpapers are stored locally and never shared
  • Location data is limited to city names only

4. Third-Party Services

ManiX may integrate with the following third-party services:

4.1 Weather Data

Weather information is fetched from public weather APIs. Your city name is only used to provide weather data and is not stored or shared with third parties.

4.2 Wallpaper Services

Wallpapers are sourced from Unsplash and other public image services. No personal data is shared with these services.

4.3 Search Engines

Search queries are sent directly to your chosen search engine (Google, Bing, etc.) and are subject to their respective privacy policies.

4.4 AI Tools and Google Apps

Quick access links to AI tools and Google apps are provided for convenience. Any interaction with these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible through Chrome's developer tools
  • Modify: You can change or delete any stored preferences through the extension settings
  • Delete: Uninstalling the extension automatically removes all stored data
  • Opt-out: You can disable specific features that collect data (weather, location, etc.)
  • Backup: Export your settings to a local file for backup purposes
  • Reset: Reset all settings to default at any time

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as you use the extension:

  • Data persists until you uninstall the extension
  • Clearing browser data may remove extension data
  • No data is retained after uninstallation
  • Backup files are stored locally and under your control

7. Permissions Required

ManiX requires the following permissions to function:

  • Storage: To save your preferences and settings locally
  • Bookmarks: To access and display your Chrome bookmarks
  • Search: To integrate with your browser's search functionality

All permissions are used only for the stated purposes and no additional data collection occurs.

8. Children's Privacy

ManiX does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting the new policy on our website
  • Notifying users through the Chrome Web Store
